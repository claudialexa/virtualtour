You can jump in your car and head 4 hours up Florida’s Turnpike to Orlando. Right in the heart of Central Florida, Orlando is home to many great theme parks such as Walt Disney World, Universal Studios and SeaWorld.

THE KEYS

You can embark on a fishing trip, go boating or kayaking, or explore one of many sunken shipwrecks in the relaxed atmosphere of the Florida Keys. The string of tropical islands offers delicious seafood, quiet beaches and shopping/people watching. (Pictured is Duval Street in Key West, a 4-hour drive from campus).

KEY BISCAYNE

Take the bridge to this island off Miami to its beautiful beaches and visit Miami Seaquarium or the Cape Florida Lighthouse (pictured) that resides on the tip of a 500-acre Bill Baggs State Park. If the beach is not for you, go to the Sony Ericsson Open that comes to the Key every winter.
